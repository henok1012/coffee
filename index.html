About Us

Who We Are?

TTK Plc is a privately held Ethiopian company specializing in the export of premium-grade Ethiopian Arabica coffee. With deep roots in Ethiopia’s agricultural heritage and a strong commitment to sustainability, we proudly connect the world to the rich flavors of Ethiopian coffee.

Founded in 1997 E.C. by three visionary Ethiopian entrepreneurs, TTK Plc began as a general import business and has since grown into a multi-sector enterprise. Today, our coffee export division stands as the company’s flagship, showcasing Ethiopia’s finest beans on the global stage.

We source directly from Ethiopia’s most renowned coffee-growing regions—Yirgacheffe, Sidama, Guji, and Limu Djimma—ensuring every bean reflects the unique richness of our land and the dedication of our farmers.
